About Parat Packaging

Our Story

Parat Packaging was born from frustration. In 2009, our founder noticed a glaring gap in the Indian market: businesses treated packaging as an afterthought, a cost center rather than a strategic asset. Products were beautiful, but their packaging was dull, generic, and ineffective. Meanwhile, startups and established enterprises alike were struggling to find partners who understood packaging holistically‚Äîfrom design thinking to production logistics to regulatory compliance.

That insight became our mission. We established Parat Packaging as the first truly comprehensive packaging partner for Indian businesses. Over the years, we've grown from a boutique design studio to a full-service packaging solutions provider, working with 800+ clients across FMCG, food & beverage, pharmaceuticals, e-commerce, and retail sectors. Today, we've delivered over 5,000 packaging solutions across 28 states in India.

But our core mission remains unchanged: to transform packaging from an afterthought into a competitive advantage. We believe that every product deserves packaging that protects it, reflects its quality, and speaks to its intended customer. That's the Parat Packaging difference.
